### 📩 Step 2: Get configuration

After activating connection through PortoGate Telegram bot, you'll receive:
- Link (starts with `vless://...`)
- Or QR code containing connection data

### ✅ Step 3: Add configuration to V2Box

**📱 Option 1: By link**
1. Copy the `vless://...` link
2. Open V2Box → tap **+** (top right corner)
3. Select **"Import from Link"**
4. Paste the link → tap **"Confirm"**

**📷 Option 2: Via QR code**
1. In Telegram bot tap **"Show QR code"** button
2. In V2Box app tap **+** → **"Scan QR Code"**
3. Point camera at QR code

### 🚀 Step 4: Activate connection

1. Make sure the profile is selected
2. Tap the large **"Start"** / **"Connect"** button
3. After connection, status will change to **"Connected"**

> 💡 **Tips**
> - We recommend enabling connection mode manually when needed
> - App works in background — can be disabled in settings if needed

Hiddify is a modern client for connecting to VLESS on Windows, macOS and Linux. Has simple interface and is compatible with PortoGate configurations.

### 📥 Step 1: Download Hiddify

Go to official website:
👉 [https://hiddify.com](https://hiddify.com)

Download suitable version for your OS:
- **Windows** (EXE or ZIP)
- **macOS** (Intel/ARM)
- **Linux** (AppImage or DEB)

### 📩 Step 2: Get link or QR code

In PortoGate Telegram bot you'll receive link like:
```
vless://...#PortoGate
```
or QR code.

### ✅ Step 3: Add configuration

**Option 1: Link import**
1. Open Hiddify
2. Click **"Import"** → **"By link"**
3. Paste `vless://...` link → Confirm

**Option 2: QR code scanning**
1. Open Hiddify
2. Click **"Import"** → **"Scan QR code"**
3. Point camera or upload image

### 🚀 Step 4: Connection

1. Select active profile
2. Click **"Start"**
3. Wait for status: **Connected**

> ⚙️ **Additional**
> - You can configure auto-start or system proxy (enabled manually)
> - App shows connection status and traffic statistics
> - Recommended not to change proxy type and transport unless you're sure

## Connection Verification

After setup, verify your connection:
1. Open your browser
2. Go to [whatismyipaddress.com](https://whatismyipaddress.com)
3. Confirm that your IP address has changed
